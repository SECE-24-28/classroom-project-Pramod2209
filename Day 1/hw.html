DNA Sequence Example


// Simple DNA base pairing function
function complementaryStrand(dna) {
    const pairs = {'A':'T', 'T':'A', 'G':'C', 'C':'G'};
    return dna.split('').map(base => pairs[base]).join('');
}

console.log(complementaryStrand("ATGC")); // Output: TACG

Chapter 1: Cell Biology

Understanding the basic unit of life - The Cell:

Cell surface area to volume ratio: SA/V = 6/r where r is radius

Mitochondrial DNA notation: mtDNA13 contains 104 = 10,000 base pairs

Key Cell Organelles

  • Nucleus - Contains genetic material
  • Mitochondria - Powerhouse of the cell
  • Ribosomes - Protein synthesis
  • Golgi Apparatus - Processing and packaging

Chapter 2: Ecology

Today's topic: Food chains and energy flow in ecosystems.

Trophic Levels

  1. Primary Producers (Plants)
  2. Primary Consumers (Herbivores)
  3. Secondary Consumers (Carnivores)
  4. Tertiary Consumers (Top Predators)
  5. Decomposers (Bacteria & Fungi)

Ecological Terms

Photosynthesis
Process by which plants convert light energy into chemical energy: 6CO2 + 6H2O → C6H12O6 + 6O2
Biodiversity
The variety of life in a particular habitat or ecosystem.
Carbon Footprint
Total greenhouse gas emissions caused by an individual, organization, or product.

Chapter 3: Human Anatomy

Learn about the human circulatory system:

Heart Rate = 60-100 beats per minute (average)

Blood Pressure: 140/90 mmHg (high) 120/80 mmHg (normal)

Major Blood Vessels

  • Arteries - Carry oxygenated blood from heart
  • Veins - Return deoxygenated blood to heart
  • Capillaries - Exchange nutrients and waste

Fun Facts

  1. The human body has approximately 100,000 km of blood vessels
  2. Red blood cells live for about 120 days
  3. An adult heart pumps about 7,500 liters of blood daily
